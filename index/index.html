ABOUT THE CANTEEN

Welcome to your school canteen portal. Here you can easily manage your daily dining experience: make a booking, explore our weekly menu, or check your digital wallet balance.

The canteen operates 5 days a week. For our intern students, we provide an automatic booking system for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Regular students are welcome to join us by making a reservation through this platform.
